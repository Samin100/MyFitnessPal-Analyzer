Valid Day Value

Your valid day value is the minimum threshold a day must have for it to be considered a valid day during analysis. If you find yourself frequently not filling out all your meals for a day, pick a valid day value closer to your TDEE, otherwise you can pick a lower valid day value.

The default valid day value is set to 1000 calories.
