GainStats

GainStats is a dashboard full of tools for helping you better understand your MyFitnessPal data. GainStats is currently being developed, but if you would like to test it out, login with your MyFitnessPal account above.

Intelligent Analysis

GainStats differentiates between incomplete and complete MyFitnessPal days by allowing you set a minimum calorie threshold. Any days below the threshold won't be taken into account meaning even if you filled out only 10 days last month you can still get an accurate analysis.

Data Visualizations

GainStats provides visualizations for your nutrient data over any period of time. From calories to macros, you can plot your data and see exactly how well you've been doing.

View details »

Nutrient Averages

GainStats provides averages for your calories and macros and even compares those averages to past averages. This lets you compare your present self's consumption habits to your past self since there's no one better to compare you to than yourself.
